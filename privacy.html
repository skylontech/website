Privacy Policy

Whimsical Weather App

Last updated: September 8, 2025

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign up, we collect authentication information provided by you or through third-party services (Google, Facebook), such as your name and email address, which is managed by Firebase Authentication.

Saved Locations

We collect and store the weather locations you save to provide personalized weather forecasts. This data is stored in the Firebase Realtime Database.

Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect any special device information beyond what is automatically collected by the standard Firebase SDKs for operational purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize our services, such as delivering weather forecasts for your saved locations.
  • To authenticate users and secure your account.
  • To communicate with you about service updates or support inquiries.

Data Storage and Security

Your authentication and personal data are securely stored and managed using Firebase Authentication and Firebase Realtime Database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties.

AI-Generated Content

The app uses AI to generate hero images. These images are created algorithmically and do not use any personal data or images from the user.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or via email.
